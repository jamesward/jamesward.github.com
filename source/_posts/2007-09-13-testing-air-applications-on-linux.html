But I wasn't able to get it to work. Then I realized that I don't really need the whole runtime to just test my AIR applications. All I need is ADL - the testing tool for AIR applications. So I gave it a try on a Salesforce.com project I'm working on: jamesw@dos:~/projects/mavericks/examples/air/AccountTracker/bin$ wine ~/flex_sdk-3_b1/bin/adl.exe salesforceTest-app.xml And to my total surprise the AIR application loaded and ran on Linux! Sweet!
